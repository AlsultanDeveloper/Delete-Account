Privacy Policy

Welcome to Sab Store. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal data is handled securely.

Data Collection and Usage

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and payment details to provide and improve our services. All data is encrypted during transmission and stored securely.

Account Deletion

You have the option to delete your account directly from within the Sab Store app. Once your account is deleted, all associated personal data will be permanently removed from our main server and cannot be recovered.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at support@sab-store.com.
